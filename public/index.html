    How to print clear vs white labels

    1. Choose background mode: “Clear” for transparent stickers on clear boards, “White label” for normal paper labels.
    2. For clear stickers: Use clear vinyl/clear sticker paper in your printer. Set background mode to “Clear”. Only the dark squares and logo will print; everything else stays transparent.
    3. For white labels: Use regular white label sheets or paper. Set background mode to “White label”. The QR will have a solid white box behind it.
    4. For print shops / flatbed printers: Export SVG, send the file, and tell them: “Print only the dark QR dots and logo on clear vinyl, no white background.”
    5. Always test scan: After printing, scan one sticker with your phone to confirm it reads instantly before doing a big batch.
