    How to print clear vs white labels

    1. Choose “Clear” for transparent stickers, “White label” for normal paper.
    2. Clear stickers: use clear vinyl paper. Only dots print.
    3. White labels: prints with a white box behind the QR.
    4. Print shops: export SVG and request “no white background”.
    5. Always test scan before mass printing.
