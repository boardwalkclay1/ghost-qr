How to Use the App

QR Studio Videos Guides Library How‑To

1. What GhostBoards QR Studio Does

GhostBoards QR Studio creates scannable QR labels for boards, wheels, parts, packaging, and customer support. Each QR can link to:

  • Care instructions
  • How‑to videos
  • Product guides
  • Custom URLs
  • Multi‑QR bundles (multiple items in one scan)

2. Creating a QR Code

  1. Enter the Target URL — this is the main link.
  2. Optional: Add a Video URL to attach a tutorial.
  3. Enter a Title (ex: “Board Care Guide”).
  4. Add Instructions (ex: cleaning steps, warnings).
  5. Click Generate QR.

The QR preview updates instantly.

3. Exporting Your QR

  • PNG — best for printing labels.
  • SVG — best for print shops and large formats.
  • Print — prints a clean 3×3 label instantly.
  • Share — AirDrop, Messages, Email, etc.

4. Using Quick Access

Quick Access shows your 5 most recent Guides and 5 most recent Videos. You can load items instantly into the QR Studio.

  • Click an item → loads it into the QR generator.
  • SHIFT‑Click → multi‑select mode.
  • Combine Selected → builds a multi‑QR bundle.
  • Clear Selection → resets multi‑select.

Multi‑QR bundles open a special viewer page:
https://ghost-qr.pages.dev/pages/multi.html?items=[...]

5. Using the Burger Menu

The burger menu (☰) gives you full control over your entire library. It includes:

  • Full list of all Guides
  • Full list of all Videos
  • Search bar for instant filtering
  • “+” button to add items to Quick Access
  • Tap an item to load it into the QR Studio

This is the fastest way to build multi‑QR bundles or load older items.

6. Managing Videos

Go to the Videos page to add and organize your how‑to videos.

  1. Enter a title (ex: “How to Clean Your Board”).
  2. Enter a category (ex: “Care”, “Setup”, “Safety”).
  3. Paste the video URL.
  4. Click Add Video.

Use the search bar to instantly filter your library.

7. Managing Guides

Go to the Guides page to create written instructions or product manuals.

  1. Enter a title (ex: “Wheel Rotation Guide”).
  2. Enter a category (ex: “Maintenance”).
  3. Write your guide content.
  4. Click Save Guide.

Search instantly by title, category, or content.

8. Best Practices

  • Always test‑scan your QR before printing batches.
  • Use SVG for print shops.
  • Use PNG for home printers.
  • Keep instructions short and clear.
  • Use categories to stay organized.
  • Use multi‑QR bundles for product kits or multi‑step guides.
