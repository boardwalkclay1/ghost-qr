How to Use the App

QR Studio Videos Guides How‑To

1. What the App Does

GhostBoards QR Studio lets you create scannable QR labels for boards, wheels, parts, and product packaging. Each QR can link to:

  • Care instructions
  • How‑to videos
  • Product guides
  • Custom URLs

2. Creating a QR Code

  1. Enter the **Target URL** — this is where the QR will send people.
  2. Optional: Add a **Video URL** to attach a tutorial or demo.
  3. Enter a **Title** (ex: “Board Care Guide”).
  4. Add **Instructions** (ex: cleaning steps, warnings, storage tips).
  5. Click **Generate QR**.

The QR preview will appear instantly.

3. Exporting Your QR

  • PNG — best for printing labels.
  • SVG — best for print shops and large formats.
  • Print — prints a clean 3×3 label instantly.

4. Managing Videos

Go to the Videos page to add and organize your how‑to videos.

  1. Enter a title (ex: “How to Clean Your Board”).
  2. Enter a category (ex: “Care”, “Setup”, “Safety”).
  3. Paste the video URL.
  4. Click **Add Video**.

Use the search bar to instantly filter your library.

5. Managing Guides

Go to the Guides page to create written instructions or product manuals.

  1. Enter a title (ex: “Wheel Rotation Guide”).
  2. Enter a category (ex: “Maintenance”).
  3. Write your guide content.
  4. Click **Save Guide**.

Search instantly by title, category, or content.

6. Best Practices

  • Always test‑scan your QR before printing batches.
  • Use **SVG** for print shops.
  • Use **PNG** for home printers.
  • Keep instructions short and clear.
  • Use categories to stay organized.
